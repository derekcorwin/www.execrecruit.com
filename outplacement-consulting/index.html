Outplacement Consulting

Business continues to undergo changes stemming from mergers, acquisitions and severe competition. Difficult personnel choices are required in department reductions and divisional reorganizations.  

Outplacement Consultants is experienced in assisting companies and individuals to effectively come with these situations. We have successfully completed group and individual one-on-one outplacement programs for companies privately owned or family businesses to Fortune 500 level. 

If your company is downsizing, reorganizing or laying you off, please find out if you are eligible for outplacement services. Our company has a history of assisting employees as they transition through employer separation, writing a new resume, practicing interviewing skills and how to negotiate for a new position. We assist in helping you find the right resources as you search for a new position such as networking and job opportunities.
